New York

I visited the city of New York in the summer of 2017. It was an amazing experience one I really enjoyed and I felt like it really broadened my horizons.

While there I Visited places such as the Brooklyn harbour, the Empire State, Grand central Stadium etc. I can not recommend New York more.


Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu was unlike any other city I had been to. The sights were incredible given the altitude I was at, the surrounding scenery was picturesque and breathtaking.

The city itself ,situated on the side of a mountain, was hard to access but was well worth the effort.
